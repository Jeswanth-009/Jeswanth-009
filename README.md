I’m a Software Engineer focused on building real-world products with a strong foundation in backend systems. I enjoy working across the stack, but my true passion lies in designing scalable systems, optimizing performance, and understanding how things work under the hood. 

Through hackathons and internships, I’ve learned how to move from idea → prototype → working product under real constraints. I value both speed of execution and depth of understanding.

**Currently, I’m focused on:**
